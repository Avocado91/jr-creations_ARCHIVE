Wallets

Keep your loose change in one of our two styles of coin purses, kiss-lock or snap closure. Lots of cards? Organize them in our kiss-lock framed card holders that can hold up to 8 cards, or our zipper version for even more cards. Giving someone a gift card? Wrap it up in one of our unique handmade gift card holder. All products can be made with a wide range of material and patterns, please contact me for specific requests.
